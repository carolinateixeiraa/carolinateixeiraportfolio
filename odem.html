Odem

Visual Arts

Odem is the Portuguese word for fear spelled backwards. This was a piece made in the Art Workshop course during secondary school, with the aim of interpreting what fear is. My classmate and I performed: she spoke softly while I screamed from a corner. The audience, blindfolded, could only hear the music and sounds we produced. We then asked the spectators to stand up and wait, telling them we were placing a pin somewhere among the seats. We asked them to sit back down — and no one did. That was how we interpreted that fear is an illusion, because the pin had been in front of them the entire time.
