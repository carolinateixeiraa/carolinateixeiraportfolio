Loquor

Graphic Design

Taking part in a clothing brand was something I always wanted to do, and at the invitation of a close friend, I became involved in designing some of the garments. It was a different kind of challenge but a deeply interesting one — understanding the brand's aesthetic and figuring out which designs would best capture the desired audience made it a process I found genuinely rewarding.
