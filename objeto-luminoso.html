Objeto Luminoso

Product Design

The brief set in the Product Design I course was to create a light-modelling object — not just another lamp, but something that would change our perception of light itself. Paper was the only permitted primary material, which felt daunting at first. In the end, I loved working on this project. It helped me better understand how an initial idea can evolve and shift throughout the process, and that it is only through experimentation that we truly discover the direction we want to take.
