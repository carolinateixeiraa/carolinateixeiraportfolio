Madrid Video

Video

A video capturing my first time in the city of Madrid — a small Christmas trip with my family. There is something about Madrid in December that feels particularly alive: the streets lit up, the warmth of the people, and the energy of a city that never quite slows down. It was a short trip, but one that left a lasting impression and, as always, made me want to pick up a camera and hold onto every moment of it.
