The Red Carpet

Visual Arts

I have had a fascination with realism from an early age, particularly portraiture. Kika Nazareth, Manuel Ugarte, and Neymar Jr. are some of the portraits I have made. The goal was to draw celebrities who, at different points in my life, inspired me in some way.
