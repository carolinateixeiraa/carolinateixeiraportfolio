Wallows Poster

Graphic Design

A project set in the first-year course Materials and Technologies of Graphic Design. We were asked to choose a pop act, and my choice was the American band Wallows — one of my favourite bands since 2020. The brief involved creating four different posters without using any photography. I decided to base each one on my four favourite albums and EPs. The final poster had a more open brief, requiring the use of photography, and I drew inspiration from the album Model. The W of Wallows is a logo I created for the band: the centre of the W represents the drummer, with the two vocalists leaning in to sing on either side.
