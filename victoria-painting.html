Victoria Painting

Visual Arts

This painting was created for my Victoria thesis. I made a jersey inspired by the players and afterwards decided to paint this canvas to frame football within the context of art. It is one of the most gratifying works I have made — especially knowing that this painting was sent to the Portuguese women's national team itself.
