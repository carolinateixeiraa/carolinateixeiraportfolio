Through the Painting

Video · Short Film

We all have dreams — some more than others. This short film is about exactly that: dreams, including ones that society may not always welcome, and that end up being forgotten. Made in the Multimedia Workshop course, during the Visual Arts programme at Salesianos de Lisboa, and screened at the El Corte Inglés cinemas, the film was awarded Best Production. It took three months to complete, but the result was deeply gratifying.
