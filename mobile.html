Mobile

Product Design

In the 3D Design Fundamentals course, we were asked to create a mobile inspired by Alexander Calder. I wanted mine to go beyond the object itself and carry a meaning. The black and white side represents how, in any situation, illusion is always present. The other side, with red circles, represents reality — and how reality always seeps in. Made from reflective adhesive material, when the mobile is displayed, the red also bleeds into the surrounding space.
