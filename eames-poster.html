Eames Poster

Graphic Design

A project set in the Graphic Design I course. We were asked to choose a designer featured in a book held in the library of the Faculty of Architecture of the University of Lisbon. My choice was Charles and Ray Eames. Using only books as research sources, we selected a quote by our chosen designer and produced three posters: the first using text only, the second allowing text and shapes, and the third allowing everything, with photography required. In my first poster, every detail carries meaning: the triangle references The Toy, a piece by this design duo; the circle references their plywood sculptures, evoking the holes found in some of them; and the connected T's reference another quote by Charles — "Connections, connections, it's all about the connections.".
