Victoria Thesis This was my final thesis for secondary school — a project where we could choose any topic we wanted. I decided to create an advertisement for a brand I founded myself, Victoria. The goal was clear: an emotionally compelling ad that drew an analogy between art and football, honouring everything the Portuguese women's national team has achieved to this day. This project was an eye-opener that helped me understand what I truly wanted to pursue professionally — and it is exactly this: moving people with advertisements that capture attention and stay in memory, working alongside the world's greatest athletes.
